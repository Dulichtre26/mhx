About Tao Dan Park

Tao Dan Park is a beloved urban park in District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, established in 1869. Covering over 10 hectares, it offers a peaceful escape with lush greenery, walking paths, and historical landmarks like the Tao Dan Pagoda. It’s a popular spot for locals and tourists to relax, exercise, and enjoy cultural events.
