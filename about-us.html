Who We Are

Du Lịch Trẻ is a dedicated team of students from Open University HCMC, participating in the Mùa Hè Xanh 2025 program. Our mission is to showcase the rich culture, history, and beauty of Ho Chi Minh City to tourists worldwide. With a passion for travel and community service, we create memorable experiences through our curated destinations and warm hospitality.
