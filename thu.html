Meet the Young Travel Team

We are the Young Travel Team, a passionate group of students from Open University HCMC, dedicated to showcasing the vibrant culture and beauty of Ho Chi Minh City. As part of the Green Summer 2025 project, our mission is to guide tourists with enthusiasm and share the hidden gems of Saigon. Join us on this exciting journey!
