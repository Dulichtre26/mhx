About Ben Thanh Market

Ben Thanh Market, located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, is one of Vietnam's most iconic markets. Established in 1912, it offers a vibrant mix of local handicrafts, textiles, souvenirs, and street food. Visitors can explore over 1,400 stalls, sample traditional Vietnamese phở, or shop for unique gifts. The market's distinctive clock tower is a beloved landmark.
